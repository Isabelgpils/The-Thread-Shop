About Our Store

Our store was established in 1992 in the Madison area to provide a wide assortment of fibers for a wide variety of crafts. We stock nearly every brand of yarn and embroidery thread. If we do not have it in stock we will order it for our customers. Come in an browse and be sure to check out our gallery of finished items created by our customers.
